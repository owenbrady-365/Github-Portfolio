About Me

Welcome! I'm Owen Brady, a 26 year old aspiring mechanical / aerospace engineer. In my lifetime, I would like to help research and develop novel electric aircraft.

I am currently enrolled as a full-time Mechanical Engnineering student at Camosun College. My Goals for my education are to graduate the diploma program with an A average, complete the UVIC bridge program, and then graduate from UVIC with a Bachelors degree in Aerospace Engineering.

This website is designed to showcase my skills, experiences, and accomplishments in the field of engineering.

My goal is to provide a comprehensive overview of my journey as an engineer, and to demonstrate my passion for innovation and problem-solving.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find the information here informative and engaging.
